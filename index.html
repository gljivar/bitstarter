Bring your classmates together

Organize yourself and your fellow classmates into learning groups based on real language school groups.

Share and communicate lessons, words and phrases

Share together lessons and what you have learned in them. Phrases from lessons, words and examples all become part of your group's dictionary. Let people from the group discuss, correct or edit these phrases and words. This becomes your group's personal dictionary notebook!

Get yourself ready for the exam

Prepare yourself for the final exam! Review all the lessons, phrases, words and discussions about them all in one place. Help a colleague that missed a lesson to easily check what you have studied. And next year, refresh your knowledge about what you have learned a year before.

Works on every device

Whether you are using a PC, Mac, iPhone, iPad, Android or a good old Linux, application works seamlessly on all of them. Put your knowledge in the cloud and access it from any device that uses an internet connection!

FAQ

Who is this application for?
Application is intended to be used by students of any foreign language.
Why are you making this application?
To overlap problems with remembering words and phrases learned over the years in foreign languages.
